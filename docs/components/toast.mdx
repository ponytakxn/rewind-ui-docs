The Toast/Snackbar component provides brief messages that appear temporarily at the bottom or top of the screen.
These notifications are used to inform users about actions they have taken or events that have occurred, such as a successful form submission or a new message received.
They typically disappear automatically after a few seconds or can be dismissed by the user, ensuring they don't obstruct the main content for long periods.
